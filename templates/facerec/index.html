STATEMENT OF THE PROBLEM

Poor Image Quality

One of the biggest challenges of face recognition technology is poor image quality, which can lead to inaccurate identification of suspects. Poor lighting conditions, blurry images, and low-resolution images can all affect the accuracy of face recognition systems.

Diversity in Faces

Another challenge associated with face recognition technology is the diversity of human faces. Different races, ages, and genders can have unique facial features, making it difficult for systems to accurately identify individuals from certain groups.

Privacy Concern

Face recognition technology raises significant privacy concerns, as it involves collecting and storing personal information without consent. This can lead to potential misuse of the technology and violation of individuals' rights

Biases

Face recognition systems can also be biased, as they can be trained on datasets that are not diverse enough, leading to inaccurate identification of individuals from certain group
